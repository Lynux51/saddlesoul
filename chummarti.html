Chummarti Horse

A rare mountain horse breed used for local travel and farming in cold regions.

Chummarti horses are known for their toughness and ability to carry loads through rugged terrain. They are a valuable breed for remote Himalayan communities.

OriginHimalayan region, India
Height12-13 hands
UseTransport, farming
TemperamentStrong, calm
