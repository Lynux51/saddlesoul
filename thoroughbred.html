Thoroughbred Horse

The fastest horse breed widely used in racing.

The Thoroughbred horse is a highly athletic breed best known for its speed and performance in horse racing.

Origin

England

Height

15 – 17 hands

Lifespan

20 – 25 years

Temperament

Energetic & Competitive

Overview

Thoroughbred horses originated in England and are bred specifically for racing performance, agility, and speed.

Physical Traits

Origin England
Height 15–17 hands
Lifespan 20–25 years
Common Colors Bay, Brown, Black
Special Feature High speed and athletic build

Common Uses

  • Horse racing
  • Show jumping
  • Eventing
  • Sport riding

Things to Check Before Buying

  • Check leg joints for injuries
  • Observe running gait
  • Inspect breathing during exercise
  • Review training history

Price & Maintenance

Thoroughbred horses can range from ₹8,00,000 – ₹50,00,000+ depending on racing pedigree.
