Punjabi Horse (Nukra)

A strong and majestic breed from Punjab, valued for riding and ceremonial use.

The Punjabi Nukra is known for its impressive build and elegant presence. It is used for riding, ceremony, and display in northern India.

OriginPunjab, India
Height15-16 hands
UseRiding, ceremonial use
TemperamentBold, proud
