Arabian Horse

One of the oldest and most famous horse breeds in the world.

The Arabian horse is known for its endurance, intelligence, and elegant appearance. It is one of the oldest horse breeds and has influenced many modern breeds.

Origin

Middle East

Height

14 – 15 hands

Lifespan

25 – 30 years

Temperament

Intelligent & Energetic

Overview

Arabian horses originated in the deserts of the Middle East. They are known for their stamina and are widely used in endurance racing and breeding programs.

Physical Traits

Origin Middle East
Height 14–15 hands
Lifespan 25–30 years
Common Colors Grey, Bay, Chestnut
Special Feature Concave facial profile

Common Uses

  • Endurance racing
  • Horse shows
  • Breeding programs
  • Recreational riding

Things to Check Before Buying

  • Check breathing capacity
  • Inspect hooves and joints
  • Observe stamina during riding
  • Check overall body structure

Price & Maintenance

Arabian horses usually cost between ₹5,00,000 – ₹25,00,000+ depending on pedigree and training.
