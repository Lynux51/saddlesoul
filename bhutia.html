Bhutia Horse

A sturdy Himalayan breed used by local communities for transport and farming.

Bhutia horses are valued for their strength and endurance in mountain environments. They are often used on narrow trails and in cold regions.

OriginHimalayan region, India
Height12-14 hands
UseTransport, agriculture, riding
TemperamentStrong, resilient
