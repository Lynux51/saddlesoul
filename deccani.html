Deccani Horse

A resilient southern Indian breed known for its endurance and hardiness.

The Deccani horse is used for riding and light draft work across the Deccan plateau. It is known for its stamina and ability to handle hot climates.

OriginDeccan Plateau, India
Height14-15 hands
UseRiding, transport
TemperamentStrong, steady
