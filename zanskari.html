Zanskari Horse

A compact mountain breed from Ladakh, ideal for steep trails and cold weather.

Zanskari horses are sure-footed and strong. They are commonly used for transport and local riding in the high Himalayas.

OriginLadakh, India
Height12-13 hands
UsePack riding, transport
TemperamentBrave, steady
