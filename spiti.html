Spiti Horse

A hardy mountain horse bred for the cold high-altitude terrain of Spiti Valley.

Spiti horses are adapted to rocky trails and chilly weather. They are reliable pack animals and local companions in the Himalayan region.

OriginSpiti Valley, India
Height12-13 hands
UsePack riding, transport
TemperamentCalm, sure-footed
