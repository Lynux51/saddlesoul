Kachchhi-Sindhi Horse

A desert-adapted horse breed from western India and Sindh region.

The Kachchhi-Sindhi horse is prized for its endurance in dry, hot climates. It is commonly used for riding and local transport.

OriginKutch & Sindh region
Height14-15 hands
UseRiding, transport
TemperamentDurable, alert
